Matrix WarZone

Gear Up, Dive In, Dominate the Zone

Description

Matrix Warzone, where you can explore the art of game and performance in this thrilling battlefield experience! Learn how to enhance your gameplay, make smarter decisions, and execute advanced tactics. Discover how effectively communicate and coordinate with your team for maximum impact in battle. Share strategies and techniques with like-minded individuals who are just as passionate about the game!

Rules & Regulations

  • Eligibility: Participate in team of 2
  • Integrity: Strictly no cheating; ethical conduct is mandatory.
  • Resources: 1 Gaming laptop with charge, headphones or earphones 50Gb minimum space
  • Judging: Based on accuracy, efficiency.
  • Prizes: Awarded to top participants; decisions by judges are final.
  • Conduct: Disruptive or disrespectful behavior leads to disqualification.
